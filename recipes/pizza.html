Pizza

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for a delicious pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Pepperoni
  • Green peppers
  • Cheese
  • Black olives

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and eggs together
  2. Knead the mixture into a dough using your hands
  3. Flatten the dough into a circle (to the size of your pizza)
  4. Fold over an inch of the circumference of the dough to make the crust
  5. Shred the cheese and sprinkle it onto the dough
  6. Cut the peppers and add it to your dough
  7. Add the olives to your dough
  8. Add the pepperoni to the dough
  9. Bake for 20 minutes at 450 degrees Farenheit
