Bread

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe of bread

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix flour and eggs together
  2. Add sugar to the mixture
  3. Add baking powder to mixture
  4. Roll the dough into desired shape
  5. Bake for 20 minutes at 450 degrees Farenheit
