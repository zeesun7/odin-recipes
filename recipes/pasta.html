Pasta

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for delicious pasta

Ingredients

  • Dry Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sausage
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Put 1L of water into a pot and boil
  2. Add pasta to the pot and boil for 8 minutes
  3. Strain the pasta
  4. Add the pasta back into the pot and add tomato sauce
  5. Chop the sausage and add it to the pasta
  6. Garnish with Parsley
